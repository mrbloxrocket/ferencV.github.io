FIRST Robotics

SOBOTZ team 6911 member for 3 years

I joined team 6911 in my freshman year, excited to dive deeper into the world of robotics. As part of the team, I took on the challenge of programming the drivetrain, which involved coordinating the movement and speed of the robot to navigate the competition field effectively. This hands-on experience allowed me to apply my coding skills in a practical setting, and I loved seeing my code come to life as the robot moved according to my instructions. Additionally, I helped program the climb mechanism, which required precise timing and coordination to ensure the robot could successfully ascend during matches. Working on these projects not only enhanced my technical skills but also taught me the importance of teamwork and collaboration in achieving our goals. ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤ

Kiwimbi and outreach

Created videos for students in Africa to teach coding

I wanted to share my passion for coding and help others develop their skills, so I designed the videos to be engaging and easy to follow.
